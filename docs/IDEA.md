# Convaire

## One-line pitch

**Convaire is a voice AI agent platform—SendGrid for AI phone calls.** Users sign in, connect telephony, define a campaign, and press start to reach numbers at scale.

## What it is

A **general-purpose AI calling toolbox**: straightforward to use, not a bespoke integration project. You get campaigns, voice AI, and carrier connectivity in one place instead of wiring Twilio, models, and orchestration yourself.

## Core user journey

1. **Sign up / log in** — Create an account and access the dashboard.
2. **Connect Twilio** — Import or link a Twilio account so calls run on your numbers and billing.
3. **Create a campaign** — Configure who to call, scripts or agent behavior, and scheduling as the product allows.
4. **Start** — Launch the campaign; the platform places calls and the AI handles conversations on the line.

## Why it exists

- **Fast path from idea to calls** — Campaign-first workflow, minimal ceremony.
- **Twilio-backed** — Real phone network via your connected account.
- **AI on the call** — Voice agents handle dialogue so teams do not build speech stacks from scratch.

## Example use cases

These are illustrations, not a fixed product boundary:

- **Sales / cold outreach** — Outbound prospecting with AI-led conversations.
- **Hiring** — Screening calls or interview scheduling at volume.
- **Reminders & notifications** — One-to-many voice touchpoints.
- **Surveys & feedback** — Structured calls that adapt to replies.
- **Support callbacks** — Follow-ups when SMS or email is not enough.

## Positioning

Convaire optimizes for **“campaign plus go”** simplicity: connect telephony, configure the agent, run calls. It is for teams that want reliable AI phone outreach without assembling a custom voice stack.
